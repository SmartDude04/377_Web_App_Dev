The Pride of Hanover

Directed by Matt Harden and assistant director Charles Morse, the Pride of Hanover Marching Band is the town of Hanover's premiere band. With about 40 members in the 2023-2024 season, the Pride of Hanover Marching band plays at all home football games during the season. Additionally, the band plays at town events, scheudled fundraisers, concerts, and other gatherings.
